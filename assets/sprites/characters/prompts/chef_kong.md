# Chef Kong

## Prompt

Create a 128×128 pixel art character sprite in classic 16-bit SNES / arcade-era style. Front-facing, centered, idle pose, transparent background. The character is "Chef Kong" — the diet-and-meals-prep master. A jovial, cocky arcade chef with a giant knife. Use chibi proportions: oversized head about 40% of total height, round friendly body, stubby limbs, big toothy grin, small black dot-eyes under a raised eyebrow.

Outfit and details: classic tall white chef's hat (mushroom-shape), white double-breasted chef's jacket with black buttons, orange neckerchief tied at the throat, white pants with thin black stripes, black shoes, a white apron tied at the waist. He is holding a large shiny cleaver-style knife in his right hand (gray blade, black handle, one-pixel yellow highlight) — held upright, not menacing, more "let's cook" than scary. The other hand can be on his hip or giving a thumbs up. A small orange carrot or orange fruit in a belt pocket is optional flair.

Use ONLY these 10 hex colors — no other shades, no anti-aliasing, no gradients:
`#000000 #FFFFFF #FFCC00 #FF3333 #00DDFF #FFB8DE #FFA63F #2121FF #9EFA00 #4A4A4A`

Primary colors should be **white (#FFFFFF)** (hat, jacket) and **orange (#FFA63F)** (neckerchief, accents). Gray (#4A4A4A) for the knife blade. Black outlines, small yellow highlights. Chunky 16-bit pixels with limited dithering to shade the chef's jacket folds and knife blade — reference: Shovel Knight, Wonder Boy in Monster World, or Street Fighter II character portraits. Hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no gradients. 1–2 pixel black outlines. Leave a few pixels of transparent margin on every side.

## Output checklist

- [ ] 128×128 px exactly
- [ ] Transparent PNG (no solid background)
- [ ] Only the 10 listed hex colors
- [ ] Tall white chef hat clearly visible
- [ ] Knife in hand (non-threatening, "let's cook" energy)
- [ ] Orange accent (neckerchief or fruit)
- [ ] Chibi proportions (big head, small body)
- [ ] Hard-edged pixels, no smoothing
