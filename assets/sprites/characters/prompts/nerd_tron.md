# Nerd Tron

## Prompt

Create a 128×128 pixel art character sprite in classic 16-bit SNES / arcade-era style. Front-facing, centered, idle pose, transparent background. The character is "Nerd Tron" — the university/study class champion. A gangly cyber-geek with glasses and a book; think 80s science-fair kid meets cyberpunk hacker. Use chibi proportions: oversized head about 40% of total height, slim gangly body, slightly slouched stance, big owlish glasses, small smug smirk.

Outfit and details: lime-green t-shirt with a cyan circuit/binary pattern on the chest (simple geometric shapes, no readable text), blue cargo pants rolled at the ankle, white sneakers, big black-rimmed square glasses covering most of the upper face with white pixel highlights for lenses, messy black hair (could have a lime-green streak). He is holding a thick book in his left hand (cyan cover with a single yellow star or lightning bolt on it), and in his right hand a pencil or stylus held up like a lightsaber. Optional: one suspender strap visible on shoulder for extra nerd flair.

Use ONLY these 10 hex colors — no other shades, no anti-aliasing, no gradients:
`#000000 #FFFFFF #FFCC00 #FF3333 #00DDFF #FFB8DE #FFA63F #2121FF #9EFA00 #4A4A4A`

Primary colors should be **lime (#9EFA00)** and **blue (#2121FF)**, with cyan (#00DDFF) on the book and chest pattern. Black outlines, white lens highlights. Chunky 16-bit pixels with dithering on the t-shirt circuit pattern and book cover — reference: Mega Man X or Castlevania: Symphony of the Night character sprites. Hard edges, no anti-aliasing, no gradients. 1–2 pixel black outlines. Leave a few pixels of transparent margin on every side.

## Output checklist

- [ ] 128×128 px exactly
- [ ] Transparent PNG (no solid background)
- [ ] Only the 10 listed hex colors
- [ ] Big square glasses clearly visible
- [ ] Book in hand with cyan cover
- [ ] Lime green t-shirt + blue pants
- [ ] Chibi proportions (big head, small body)
- [ ] Hard-edged pixels, no smoothing
