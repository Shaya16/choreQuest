# Sweepman

## Prompt

Create a 128×128 pixel art character sprite in classic 16-bit SNES / arcade-era style. Front-facing, centered, idle pose, transparent background. The character is "Sweepman" — the household-chores hero. Think a mid-80s arcade janitor who is secretly the most powerful person in the game. Use chibi proportions: oversized head about 40% of total height, sturdy compact body, short legs, determined expression with a tiny smirk.

Outfit and details: blue jumpsuit/overalls with a gray t-shirt underneath, rolled-up sleeves showing small muscled forearms, yellow rubber gloves, work boots, a gray flat cap or backwards baseball cap. He is holding a wooden broom upright in his right hand like a staff, the bristles bright yellow. Optional: a small spray bottle clipped to his belt. Pose: one hand on the broom, the other hand in a small "let's get to work" clench. Stance slightly wide, confident.

Use ONLY these 10 hex colors — no other shades, no anti-aliasing, no gradients:
`#000000 #FFFFFF #FFCC00 #FF3333 #00DDFF #FFB8DE #FFA63F #2121FF #9EFA00 #4A4A4A`

Primary colors should be **blue (#2121FF)** and **gray (#4A4A4A)**, with yellow (#FFCC00) on the gloves and broom bristles as the pop accent. Black outlines, white highlights. Chunky 16-bit pixels with light 2-color dithering on the denim folds and broom handle — reference: Super Ghouls 'n Ghosts or Streets of Rage. No anti-aliasing, no gradients, no modern flat-retro. 1–2 pixel black outlines. Leave a few pixels of transparent margin on every side of the canvas.

## Output checklist

- [ ] 128×128 px exactly
- [ ] Transparent PNG (no solid background)
- [ ] Only the 10 listed hex colors
- [ ] Blue overalls + gray undershirt clearly visible
- [ ] Broom held in hand, yellow bristles
- [ ] Chibi proportions (big head, small body)
- [ ] Hard-edged pixels, no smoothing
