# Vibe Queen

## Prompt

Create a 128×128 pixel art character sprite in classic 16-bit SNES / arcade-era style. Front-facing, centered, idle pose, transparent background. The character is "Vibe Queen" — a dance-aerobics queen radiating rhythm and energy. Use chibi proportions: oversized head about 40% of the total height, slim body, expressive face with a playful wink or half-smile.

Outfit and details: bright pink leotard/crop-top with a cyan belt, pink leg warmers over white sneakers, cyan headphones over her ears with a visible coiled cord, long flowing hair in solid pink with yellow highlights on top (styled like a pony-tail or high ponytail with a cyan scrunchie). One hand on her hip, the other hand raised making a peace sign or finger gun. Subtle diagonal lean to suggest movement. Wide bright eyes with white highlights — vibe, not sultry.

Use ONLY these 10 hex colors — no other shades, no anti-aliasing, no gradients:
`#000000 #FFFFFF #FFCC00 #FF3333 #00DDFF #FFB8DE #FFA63F #2121FF #9EFA00 #4A4A4A`

Primary colors should be **pink (#FFB8DE)** and **cyan (#00DDFF)**, with black outlines and small white highlights. Use yellow (#FFCC00) sparingly for hair streaks or accents. Hard-edged chunky pixels with limited 2-color dithering for soft transitions on the leotard, hair, and leg warmers — think Gunstar Heroes or Dynamite Headdy character sprites, with SNES/Genesis-tier detail. Every pixel must be crisp; no anti-aliasing, no gradients. 1–2 pixel black outlines. Leave a few pixels of transparent margin on every side.

## Output checklist

- [ ] 128×128 px exactly
- [ ] Transparent PNG (no solid background)
- [ ] Only the 10 listed hex colors
- [ ] Pink leotard + cyan headphones clearly visible
- [ ] Idle pose with attitude (hip, peace sign, or lean)
- [ ] Chibi proportions (big head, small body)
- [ ] Hard-edged pixels, no smoothing
