# Gym Fighter

## Prompt

Create a 128×128 pixel art character sprite in classic 16-bit SNES / arcade-era style. Front-facing, centered, idle pose, transparent background. The character is "Gym Fighter" — a muscle-bound arcade brawler who lives in the gym. Use chibi proportions: oversized head about 40% of the total height, broad blocky torso, short stubby limbs, big confident smirk.

Outfit and details: red tank top showing bulky yellow-highlighted biceps, yellow sweatband across the forehead, fingerless black boxing gloves, red shorts with a yellow stripe, white sneakers with black laces. He is holding a single black dumbbell in his right hand, resting on his shoulder. Pose is slightly turned with the chest puffed out, one eyebrow raised, teeth visible in a cocky grin.

Use ONLY these 10 hex colors — no other shades, no anti-aliasing, no gradients:
`#000000 #FFFFFF #FFCC00 #FF3333 #00DDFF #FFB8DE #FFA63F #2121FF #9EFA00 #4A4A4A`

Primary colors should be **red (#FF3333)** and **yellow (#FFCC00)**, with black outlines and small white highlights on the muscles and teeth. Pixels are chunky and hard-edged, with limited 2-color dithering allowed for shadow and highlight transitions on the biceps, shorts, and dumbbell. Think Street Fighter II or Final Fight character portraits — 16-bit arcade crispness, not modern retro-inspired vector art. 1–2 pixel black outlines, slightly heavier on the silhouette edge. Leave a few pixels of transparent margin on every side of the canvas.

## Output checklist

- [ ] 128×128 px exactly
- [ ] Transparent PNG (no solid background)
- [ ] Only the 10 listed hex colors
- [ ] Red tank top, yellow sweatband clearly visible
- [ ] Dumbbell in right hand
- [ ] Chibi proportions (big head, small body)
- [ ] Hard-edged pixels, no smoothing
